ABOUT

Hello, I’m Carlos. I enjoy design, not so much writing things down. But here’s a little about myself. I am Cuban-American, raised in Florida, currently reside in Oregon. With aspirations of moving to Japan. In my past lives, I was a bartender, auto painter, martial artist, and Viking.

With an insatiable curiosity, I’m always looking for a challenge and an opportunity to learn. All my seemingly disconnected experiences have shaped my perspective in a way that often makes me feel like an outsider. And I hope that never changes.
