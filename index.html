ABOUT

Hello, I’m Carlos. Cuban-American, raised in Florida, living in Oregon. With aspirations of moving to Japan. In my past lives, I was a bartender, auto painter, martial artist, and Viking. The only consistent aspect of my life has been my love for art and design.

After escaping a cult, I became fascinated with how people think and behave. I believe thoughtful design can improve our lives in unexpected and meaningful ways. Im always stoked when I open a door properly on the first try.

I took a lot of detours getting into the design field. But all those experiences shaped my perspective of the world in ways I dont believe are common in the industry…although im sure many people would say the same thing. But this is my website!
